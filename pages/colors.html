Contextual Colors and Backgrounds
Klasify also has some contextual classes that can be used to provide "meaning through colors". The classes for text colors are: .color-dark-blue, .color-teal, .color-light-teal, .color-yellow, .color-orange, .color-red, .color-white, and .color-black:
